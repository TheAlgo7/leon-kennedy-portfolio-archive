About Me

Case Background

Leon S. Kennedy entered Raccoon City on September 28, 1998 — his first shift as an RPD rookie. The city fell within hours. He didn't. Recruited by the U.S. government in the aftermath, he spent the next two decades as a blade pointed at problems no one else could solve. Currently operates under DSO directive. Status: active. Mental condition: classified.
