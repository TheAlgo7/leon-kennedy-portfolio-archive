## Design Context

### Users
Leon Kennedy fan/portfolio archive site — primarily viewed by Resident Evil fans, game enthusiasts, and the developer's own portfolio audience. Consumed in browser, likely at night or in low-light, as a showcase/time-capsule piece.

### Brand Personality
Tired, precise, darkly funny. Like a classified government document that somehow developed a sense of humour about itself.

### Aesthetic Direction
Near-black tactical dark mode. Blood red (#cc0000) as the sole accent. No warmth, no friendliness — controlled precision. Anti-references: anything soft, pastel, or "cool developer portfolio." The character is not a hero — he's a weapon asking what it cost him. The design should feel that way too.

### Design Principles
1. Restraint over spectacle — every effect earns its place or it's cut
2. Dark by default, always — this dossier was never meant to be read in daylight
3. Character specificity — no copy or label that could belong to anyone else
4. Tactical precision — clean geometry, tight type, no decorative noise
5. The human cost shows — the "0% Normal Life" is as important as the 100% marksmanship
